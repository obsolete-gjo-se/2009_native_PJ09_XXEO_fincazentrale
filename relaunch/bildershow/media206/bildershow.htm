Last Year

@media 2005 was a huge success. 350 people enjoyed presentations from the likes of Jeffrey Zeldman, Joe Clark, Molly Holzschlag, and Doug Bowman, and had a unique opportunity to mingle with other likeminded, progressive web designers, who came not only from across the length and breadth of the UK, but from Europe and even further afield.
